FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

What are your hours and do you offer early or late appointments? +
Yes — we open at 7:30 AM and stay open until 7:00 PM Monday through Friday, and are open Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. These hours are specifically designed for Downtown Manhattan professionals who can't easily step away during a typical workday. Early morning and evening slots fill up quickly, so we recommend booking in advance.
What insurance plans do you accept? +
We participate in or accept most union and major dental insurance plans including Aetna, BlueCross BlueShield, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, MetroPlus Health Plan, Medicaid, and many more. Please call our office at (212) 732-7400 to verify your specific plan before your visit.
Where exactly is 225 Broadway and how do I get there? +
We're located at 225 Broadway, Suite 101 in the heart of the Financial District — steps from Wall Street, City Hall, the World Trade Center, and One World Trade. We're easily accessible via Fulton St (A/C/J/Z/2/3/4/5 trains), Cortlandt St (1/R/W), and Chambers St (2/3). Many of our patients walk from their office buildings.
Do you offer emergency dental care? +
Yes. We accommodate same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental issues including severe tooth pain, broken or chipped teeth, lost fillings, and other dental emergencies. Call us at (212) 732-7400 as early as possible and let us know it's urgent — we will do everything we can to see you the same day.
Do you see children as well as adults? +
Absolutely. We offer pediatric dentistry alongside our full range of adult dental services, making us a convenient one-stop practice for families in Downtown Manhattan, Tribeca, and the surrounding neighborhoods. Our staff is experienced with young patients and creates a calm, welcoming environment for children.
